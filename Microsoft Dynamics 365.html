Microsodt Dynamics 365

In this course, you will learn the following topics:

Introduction to Microsoft Dynamics 365
Understand what Dynamics 365 is, including its benefits and use cases in businesses.
Overview of Dynamics 365 Modules
Learn about modules like Sales, Customer Service, Field Service, Finance, and more.
Setting up Dynamics 365 Environment
Steps to provision, configure, and access a Dynamics 365 environment.
Data Management in Dynamics 365
Managing entities, records, imports/exports, and data integrity.
Customization and Configuration
How to tailor forms, views, fields, and business rules to your needs.
Security and User Roles
Define roles, privileges, and security models to manage access control.
Dynamics 365 Reporting and Analytics
Creating dashboards, charts, and reports to analyze data effectively.
Integration with Microsoft Products
Connecting Dynamics with Power BI, Teams, SharePoint, and Office 365.
Dynamics 365 Automation and Workflows
Build automated flows using Power Automate and classic workflows.
Troubleshooting and Maintenance
Monitor system health, resolve issues, and follow maintenance best practices.
Dynamics 365 Development
Learn about JavaScript, custom plugins, and PowerApps development.
Dynamics 365 Deployment and Upgrades
Best practices for deploying solutions and upgrading environments.
Best Practices and Performance Tuning
Optimize your Dynamics instance for speed, reliability, and scale.
Integration with External Systems
Use APIs, Azure Logic Apps, and custom connectors to connect to third-party apps.
Final Project
Apply all topics in a mini-project that simulates a real Dynamics CRM scenario.
